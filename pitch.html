03 Why we build a

FRIENDSLOP GAME.

Social gaming is shifting from winning to what happens between players. The most memorable games aren't the most complex — they're the ones that create stories.

  1. 1. Chaotic but accessible.

    Easy to pick up. Hard to keep it together. Skill ceiling is how well you laugh together.

  2. 2. Designed for shareable failure.

    Every flight produces a "you had to be there" moment worth retelling.

  3. 3. The game does the marketing.

    Vertical chaos for TikTok, screams for YouTube reactions, clips for Discord.

05
— THE GAMEPLAY LOOP —

Four verbs. Every flight.

Every player is doing one of these at any moment — and probably failing at it. The game emerges from how the four blur into each other.

GATEVERBWHAT IT MEANS
01 SERVE Coffee, snacks, smiles. Keep passenger satisfaction green.
02 REPAIR Wings, fuel lines, cabin pressure. Tools, hands, panic.
03 DEFEND Birds, asteroids, smuggled livestock, angry passengers.
04 FLY Someone has to land this thing. Anyone. Please.
