FRIENDSLOP.

Social gaming is shifting from winning to what happens between players. The most memorable games aren't the most complex — they're the ones that create stories.

  1. i. Chaotic but accessible. Easy to pick up. Hard to keep it together. Skill ceiling = how well you laugh together.
  2. ii. Designed for shareable failure. Every flight produces a "you had to be there" moment worth retelling.
  3. iii. The game does the marketing. Vertical chaos for TikTok, screams for YouTube reactions, clips for Discord.
