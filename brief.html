01

Flight manifesto

An ultra-low-cost airline where everything breaks at the same time. The players are the Banana Airlines crew. The job: get from A to B while keeping passenger happiness as high as possible — even though the cabin is on fire, the wing is leaking fuel, and someone in row 12 is, in fact, a bomb.

  • Visible happiness meters. Each passenger reacts in real time. Chaos, impacts and rough landings drag satisfaction down — and once it bottoms out, that passenger is a problem of its own.
  • Repair with whatever's around. Patch the fuselage with wood and nails. Use an umbrella as a lightning rod. Hang from a rope outside the plane to fix an engine.
  • Comedy of process, not jokes. The humor lives in the systems: "repair a door with planks while a passenger asks if the coffee has plant-based milk."

02

Gameplay loop

Four pillars · always running in parallel
P-01

Serve

  • Food, water, oxygen masks
  • Seat changes for difficult passengers
  • Lollipops for crying babies
  • Handle complaints before they spread panic
P-02

Repair

  • Doors with wood & nails
  • Engines while dangling on a rope
  • Clogged toilets with the plunger
  • Wings patched with duct tape
P-03

Defend

  • Bomb puzzles inside luggage — crack the code
  • Alien invasions in the cabin
  • Missiles from the war zone below
  • Eject threats through the door (gently)
P-04

Fly

  • Take the controls in the cockpit
  • Dodge missiles and storms
  • Respond to "TERRAIN PULL UP" alerts
  • Stick a precision landing
03

Events & threats

9 escalating threat classes
ALT-01

Turbulence

T1 → T2 → T3

Tier 1: cabin shake. Tier 2: a door gets ripped off. Tier 3: engine damage. Hold onto something.

ELX-02

Storm / Turbostorm

Lightning

Lightning strikes damage the plane. Deploy the umbrella as a lightning rod — but never open it inside the cabin.

WAR-03

War zone

Missiles

Missiles incoming from the ground. Evade, absorb, repair. Whichever happens first.

EMR-04

Bomb puzzle

Code-cracking

Codes hidden across the luggage. Find them, enter them, defuse it. Difficulty escalates per tier.

BIO-05

Alien invasion

Infection

An alien pathogen spreads across passengers. Apply antidote — or eject through the door.

BUG-06

Face bug

Vision blocked

A bug latches onto a player's face. They can't see. A teammate has to pry it off. Funny once. Funnier twice.

PWR-07

Electrical failure

Cascade

Lights flicker, doors malfunction, panic spreads. Reset the fuses before the cabin loses it.

GPW-08

TERRAIN PULL UP

Imminent impact

The alarm screams. Someone has to sprint to the controls and pull up before the plane meets the dirt.

WC-09

Clogged bathroom

Plunger ops

The toilet is overflowing. The plunger is in your hands. Turbulence is making it splash. Good luck.

05

Passengers

Each one carries their own happiness meter

Every passenger reacts in real time. Quick service, masks during turbulence and the right candy push their meter up; luggage to the head, rough landings, and watching the person in the next seat get sucked out a door push it down. Happy passengers tip. Unhappy passengers spread panic.

12A

Crying baby

Tantrum at altitude. A single lollipop solves it.

07F

Smelly passenger

Neighbors are losing happiness. Move them or open a window — figuratively.

22C

Suction hazard

Sitting next to a compromised door. Move them now.

14B

Hit by luggage

Apologize. Lollipop. Maybe both.

19D

Infected passenger

Antidote, or eject. Do not stall.

01A

Panicked passenger

Spreads panic to the row. Calm them before it cascades.
