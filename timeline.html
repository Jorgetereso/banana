Milestone checklist

Definition of done
M0 · end W2 · May 3

Scope lock

GDD v1 signed. Prototype feature list frozen. Unity project + repo live. Asset naming + pipeline doc. Discord/Slack ready.

M1 · end W4 · May 17

Art bible

Style frames, hero crew turnaround, passenger silhouettes, brand kit, prop pack concepts — all approved. Ready for 3D.

M2 · end W8 · Jun 14

First playable

Hero crew rigged and moving in cabin blockout. Attend loop playable end-to-end with placeholder art. Happiness meter reacts.

M3 · end W12 · Jul 12

PROTOTYPE

Vertical slice: 1 hero crew, textured cabin, Attend + Repair loops, final HUD, scratch VO, local co-op (shared screen). Steam build.

Scope guardrails

What we're NOT doing in prototype

Netcode

Prototype is local/shared-screen. True online co-op is a post-prototype epic.

Defend & Fly pillars

Only Attend + Repair in prototype. Defend (aliens, bombs) and Fly (piloting) come after M3.

Character variety

1 fully-rigged hero crew in prototype. Other 3 are concepted but not production-ready yet.

Final VO

Scratch VO (self-recorded) for prototype. Real talent booked only for hero lines by W11.

Passenger variety

Shared base mesh with material swaps. No unique rigs or barks per passenger yet.

Steam storefront polish

Steam page is a placeholder. Capsule art, trailer, and wishlist push are post-prototype.
